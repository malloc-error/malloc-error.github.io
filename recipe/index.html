Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Asparagus

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in pan
  2. Fry garlic
  3. Add asparagus and soy sauce and mirin
  4. Stir for 2 min over high heat
