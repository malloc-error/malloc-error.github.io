Stir Fry Veggies

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Broccoli
  • Water Chestnut
  • Carrot
  • Eggplant
  • Soy Sauce

Directions

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker
  2. Cut vegetables into thin slices
  3. Sautee vegetables in pan over high heat
  4. Plate the rice and vegetables, add soy sauce
